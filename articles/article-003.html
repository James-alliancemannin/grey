Black Triangles Over Water

Silent giants that move where no aircraft should — and vanish into the deep.

March 22, 2026
Archive Index • Government • Aerial Phenomena • Level 7 Clearance

Classification: VISUAL — Restricted Distribution

Since the early 2000s, clusters of reports describe large (estimated 100–300 ft span), perfectly triangular craft operating at low altitude over oceans, lakes, and coastal zones. No visible propulsion, no exhaust, no sonic signature. They hover, accelerate instantaneously, and submerge or disappear vertically into water without splash or wake.

The Aquatic Preference

Unlike land-based sightings, oceanic triangles exhibit prolonged loiter times — sometimes hours — before descending into the water. Sonar anomalies follow: transient contacts at extreme depths, rapid movement inconsistent with known submersibles. Naval logs (redacted portions released via FOIA) describe "unidentified submersible objects" matching triangular profiles on surface radar before dive.

Key Incidents — Timeline

  • 2004–2005 — USS Nimitz / USS Princeton: Tic Tac precursors included triangular formations over Pacific. Pilots reported "hovering then instantaneous acceleration."
  • 2014–2019 — East Coast "Gimbal/GoFast" events overlapped with triangular sightings off Virginia Beach and Cape Cod — over water, low and slow.
  • 2021 — Multiple civilian videos from fishing vessels in the Gulf of Mexico: black triangles descending into water, no wake.
  • 2024–2025 — Increased reports from commercial shipping lanes in South China Sea and North Atlantic. Coordinated "swarm" behavior observed.

Physics Defiance

No known human aircraft — drone, stealth bomber, or otherwise — can maintain perfect silence, zero heat signature, and trans-medium capability (air to water) at these scales. Radar returns are intermittent or absent; infrared shows cold or null. Official statements cycle between "drones," "balloons," "optical illusions" — none explain the consistent geometry or behavior.

“It wasn't flying. It was defying.” — Retired Navy pilot, anonymous interview, 2025

Open Questions

  • Why predominantly over water — entry/exit points, or observation of subsurface activity?
  • What explains the lack of wake or disturbance on submersion?
  • Are these scouts, probes, or something stationed long-term?
  • If terrestrial — which program has hidden trans-medium triangles for decades?
